## What is Stratum?

Stratum is a decentralized **SocialFi** platform that empowers asset managers to create and run investment pools (called vaults) for strategies like yield farming, perpetual trading, and token swaps. Stratum gives investors a hassle-free way to watch their money grow, as smart managers handle the details to deliver solid returns straight to your account.
