# How to Deposit

Depositing funds into Stratum vaults is simple and secure. This guide will walk you through the entire process step by step.

## Getting Started

<Card title="Prerequisites" icon="check-circle">
Before depositing, ensure you have:
- A compatible wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
- Sufficient funds in your wallet
- Basic understanding of gas fees
- Knowledge of the vault you want to invest in
</Card>

## Step-by-Step Deposit Process

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
<Step title="Access the Platform">
Visit [monad.strtm.xyz](https://monad.strtm.xyz/) and click "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Your Wallet">
Choose from supported wallets like MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other compatible options.
</Step>

<Step title="Authorize Connection">
Approve the connection request in your wallet to link it to Stratum.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Choose a Vault

<Card title="Vault Selection" icon="search">
Browse available vaults and consider:
- Investment strategy and risk level
- Historical performance
- Management fees
- Minimum deposit requirements
- Current TVL (Total Value Locked)
</Card>

<Columns cols={2}>
<Card title="Yield Farming Vaults" icon="seedling">
- Lower risk, steady returns
- Automated yield optimization
- Suitable for conservative investors
- Regular income generation
</Card>

<Card title="Trading Vaults" icon="chart-line">
- Higher risk, higher potential returns
- Active trading strategies
- Suitable for risk-tolerant investors
- Variable returns based on market conditions
</Card>
</Columns>

### Step 3: Review Vault Details

<Card title="Due Diligence" icon="magnifying-glass">
Before depositing, review:
- Vault manager's track record
- Strategy description and risk factors
- Fee structure (management and performance fees)
- Historical performance data
- Minimum and maximum deposit limits
</Card>

### Step 4: Enter Deposit Amount

<Card title="Deposit Configuration" icon="dollar-sign">
- Enter your desired deposit amount
- Check minimum deposit requirements
- Review estimated gas fees
- Confirm you have sufficient balance
</Card>

<Warning>
**Gas Fees**: Remember that you'll need additional funds for gas fees on top of your deposit amount.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Approve and Deposit

<Steps>
<Step title="Approve Token Spending">
If depositing ERC-20 tokens, first approve the vault contract to spend your tokens.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Deposit Transaction">
Review the transaction details and confirm the deposit in your wallet.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain (usually 1-2 minutes).
</Step>

<Step title="Verify Deposit">
Check your vault balance to confirm the deposit was successful.
</Step>
</Steps>
