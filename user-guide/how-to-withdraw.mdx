# How to Withdraw

Withdrawing funds from Stratum vaults is straightforward and secure. This guide covers everything you need to know about the withdrawal process.

## Understanding Withdrawals

<Card title="Withdrawal Basics" icon="arrow-right-from-bracket">
Withdrawals allow you to:
- Retrieve your original investment
- Claim your earned profits
- Exit positions when needed
- Rebalance your portfolio
</Card>

### Withdrawal Types

<Columns cols={2}>
<Card title="Partial Withdrawal" icon="minus">
- Withdraw a portion of your funds
- Keep remaining investment active
- Maintain position in the vault
- Flexible amount control
</Card>

<Card title="Full Withdrawal" icon="x">
- Withdraw all your funds
- Close your position completely
- Exit the vault entirely
- Claim all accumulated returns
</Card>
</Columns>

## Step-by-Step Withdrawal Process

### Step 1: Access Your Vault

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
Ensure your wallet is connected to the Stratum platform.
</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Your Vault">
Go to your vault dashboard and locate your investment position.
</Step>

<Step title="Review Current Balance">
Check your current vault balance and available withdrawal amount.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Initiate Withdrawal

<Card title="Withdrawal Configuration" icon="cog">
- Select withdrawal type (partial or full)
- Enter withdrawal amount
- Review gas fees
- Confirm you have sufficient balance
</Card>

<Warning>
**Withdrawal Limits**: Some vaults may have withdrawal limits or cooldown periods. Check the vault details before proceeding.
</Warning>

### Step 3: Confirm Transaction

<Steps>
<Step title="Review Details">
Double-check the withdrawal amount, gas fees, and destination address.
</Step>

<Step title="Approve Transaction">
Confirm the withdrawal transaction in your wallet.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Processing">
Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify Withdrawal">
Check your wallet balance to confirm the funds have been received.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawal Fees and Costs

### Fee Structure

<Card title="Withdrawal Costs" icon="dollar-sign">
- **Gas Fees**: Network transaction costs
- **No Platform Fees**: Stratum doesn't charge withdrawal fees
- **Slippage**: Price impact for large withdrawals
- **Network Congestion**: Higher fees during busy periods
</Card>

### Cost Optimization

<Card title="Minimize Costs" icon="percent">
- Withdraw during low network activity
- Consider batching multiple operations
- Use appropriate gas settings
- Monitor gas price trends
</Card>
